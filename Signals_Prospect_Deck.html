How it works

Overlay, not replace.

Signals reads the cameras you already run: read-only, low-bandwidth, alongside your existing recorder, which keeps doing its job untouched. Processing happens on site, on a small box we manage. Answers arrive as a live dashboard and alerts to the right phone.
