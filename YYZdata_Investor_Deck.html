Why now

Three doors just opened.

Regulation
Privacy is a moat now
FTC actions and BIPA-class laws punish identity surveillance. Aggregate-only by design skips the entire fight, and incumbents cannot follow without abandoning their feature lists.
Economics
Edge got cheap
An Apache-licensed detector on a $300 box, no cloud inference, no video storage. A cost floor cloud-era incumbents cannot reach.
Incumbents
Their revenue is our wedge
Solink ($175/mo, video-first, 18,000+ locations) and Verkada (per-camera hardware and licenses) monetize storage and gear. Riding a customer's existing cameras cannibalizes them. It is our whole model.

The moat

The demo is the door. The data is the moat.

Any single feature can be cloned. The compounding asset cannot:
Flywheel
Licensed venue data
Every site signs an explicit, paid-for training license. Every venue improves the model the next venue buys. Incumbents' contracts and stored-video liabilities make this hard to retrofit.
Architecture
Privacy-native, edge-native
Aggregate-only from the first commit, processing on site. This is a design, not a setting, and it clears compliance review in one conversation.
Layer
The integration seam
Video plus POS, reservations, labor and sensors in one reasoning layer. The more systems connected, the harder we are to remove.
The vision

Every signal. One console.

Cameras are only the first signal, because they are the richest one a business already produces. The same layer is built to ingest anything that describes how an operation actually runs: IoT and sensors, transactions, documents and text, sound. Prevention, prediction, forecasting and execution from one console.
Philosophy
The system bends to the business
AI removes the old excuse for forcing businesses to change their process to fit software. We build to your process, your rules, your vocabulary. Best practices are suggested, never imposed.
Consequence
Verticals are configurations
A lounge, a dispensary, a showroom and a concourse are the same problem at different scales. One platform, retargeted by connector and persona, not rebuilt per industry.
Endgame
The operating layer
Not another ERP, POS or CRM. The intelligence on top of all of them: their systems stay the system of record, ours becomes the system of action.
