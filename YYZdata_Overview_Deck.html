Signals · the bigger vision

Your space, talking.

Signals is ambient intelligence for a physical operation. It starts with the cameras you already have, then extends to any signal that describes how the place actually runs. One question, asked of everything: what is happening here, and what should we do about it? Privacy-first: no biometrics, no raw video leaving the box.
Phase 1 · now
Vision
Queues and wait times, security awareness, and guest experience from existing cameras.
Phase 2 · next
Sensors & IoT
Environmental, equipment, and access data fused with what the cameras see.
Phase 3 · vision
Text & systems
Documents, POS and PMS, and CRM and other data pools, so you can ask anything of your operation.
